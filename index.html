Meet Fahmi Ibrahim

I've dedicated over two decades to soccer, developing my skills and passion through esteemed academies like De Anza Force and San Jose Earthquakes. I played collegiate soccer at UC Berkeley and Duke University, and my professional experience includes the San Jose Earthquakes' first team, Detroit Mercy, and Colorado Hailstorm FC.

Meet Fahmi Ibrahim

Why Soccer?

Soccer isn't just a sport for me; it's a lifelong passion that I've been fortunate to turn into a career. Now, as a personal trainer, I aim to recycle my knowledge and pass it on to the next generation. I am deeply passionate about teaching, coaching, mentoring, and fitness. I believe in the power of sports to transform lives and am committed to helping others achieve their goals both on and off the field. Whether it's through individualized training sessions or motivational mentoring, my goal is to inspire and empower athletes to reach their full potential.
