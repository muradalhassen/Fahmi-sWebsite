Meet Fahmi Ibrahim

I've dedicated over two decades to playing soccer, honing my skills and passion for the game. My journey has taken me through several esteemed academies in the Bay Area, including De Anza Academy and the San Jose Earthquakes Academy. I had the privilege of playing collegiate soccer at UC Berkeley and Duke University, where I developed both my athletic and academic skills.

Meet Fahmi Ibrahim

Why Soccer?

My professional career includes the San Jose Earthquakes' first team, Detroit Mercy, Colorado Hailstorm FC, and more. Soccer isn't just a sport for me; it's a lifelong passion that I've been fortunate to turn into a career. Now, as a personal trainer, I aim to recycle my knowledge and pass it on to the next generation. I am deeply passionate about teaching, coaching, mentoring, and fitness. I believe in the power of sports to transform lives and am committed to helping others achieve their goals both on and off the field. Whether it's through individualized training sessions or motivational mentoring, my goal is to inspire and empower athletes to reach their full potential.
