Meet the Founder

Meet Fahmi Ibrahim

Fahmi has dedicated over two decades to soccer, developing his skills and passion through esteemed academies like De Anza Force and the San Jose Earthquakes. He played collegiate soccer at UC Berkeley and Duke University, and his professional experience includes the San Jose Earthquakes' first team, Detroit Mercy, and Colorado Hailstorm FC.

MY STORY

Why Soccer?

For Fahmi, soccer is more than just a sport; it's a lifelong passion that he has successfully transformed into a career. Now, as a personal trainer, he aims to share his extensive knowledge with the next generation. Fahmi is deeply passionate about teaching, coaching, mentoring, and fitness. He believes in the power of sports to transform lives and is committed to helping others achieve their goals both on and off the field. Whether through individualized training sessions or motivational mentoring, his goal is to inspire and empower athletes to reach their full potential.
